About Us

The team is a group of students from Drexel University that work on the website for a project our freshman year

The project's intentions were to make a website that allowed users to find, view and share music that suits them the best

The team consists of Peter Tzimoulis, Ilysia Krzywonos, David Calamaro, and Richard Lee.


Peter Tzimoulis:

Project leader and front and backend developer who has worked on the spotify api integration and accounts


Ilysia Krzywonos:

Lead backend developer who has focus on full integration of the spotify api on the website including the playlist and social aspect from spotify


David Calamaro:

Full stack developer who has focus on integrating the music to play from the website from spotify embed and also supplying stats on the song and users


Richard Lee

Lead front end developer who has focus on the css of the overall site and kept the project with a modern look on every step of the road
